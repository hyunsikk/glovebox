## Description (4000 chars max)
Glovebox is the simplest way to track car maintenance for every vehicle you own. No account needed — just add your car and start logging.

**YOUR GARAGE, ORGANIZED**
• Track multiple vehicles in one place
• See real-time maintenance health at a glance
• Favorite your daily driver for quick access
• Store VIN, location, and vehicle documents

**SMART MAINTENANCE SCHEDULES**
• Manufacturer-recommended service intervals for 1,000+ vehicles
• Coverage from 1976 to 2026, across 41 brands and 508 models
• Get alerts when services are due or overdue
• Snooze reminders when you're not ready

**LOG EVERY SERVICE**
• Quick-log oil changes, tire rotations, brake jobs, and more
• Track cost, mileage, vendor, and notes for each service
• Attach up to 5 photos per service (receipts, parts, before/after)
• Search and filter your complete service history

**INSIGHTS THAT SAVE MONEY**
• Monthly spending trends with visual charts
• Cost per mile tracking across all vehicles
• Overdue service alerts color-coded by urgency
• Export your data anytime as JSON

**LEARN CAR CARE**
• 50+ expert articles across 14 maintenance categories
• Engine care, brakes, tires, transmission, electrical, and more
• Estimated read times so you can learn on your schedule

**PRIVACY FIRST**
• No account required — ever
• All data stored locally on your device
• No tracking, no ads in Pro, no data collection
• Export your data whenever you want

---

Whether you drive one car or five, Glovebox helps you stay on top of maintenance so your vehicles last longer and cost less. Download now and never miss a service again.
