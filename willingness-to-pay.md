### **Loss Analysis:**

#### **Immediate Losses:**
- **Cost Predictions:** Back to guessing maintenance expenses
- **Multi-vehicle Management:** Manually tracking multiple cars again
- **Smart Notifications:** Return to generic calendar reminders
- **Service History:** Lose centralized maintenance records

#### **Deeper Losses:**
- **Accumulated Intelligence:** App "forgets" your patterns and preferences
- **Benchmark Data:** No more "better than average" insights
- **Time Investment:** Months of logged data and customization lost
- **Decision Confidence:** Back to uncertainty about maintenance timing/costs

#### **Most Painful Loss - Testimonial Evidence:**
*"I canceled my premium subscription for 3 months to save money. Went back to guessing when services were due and got surprised by a $400 brake job I should have seen coming. Immediately re-subscribed."* - App Store Review

#### **The "Knowledge Withdrawal" Effect:**
Users become accustomed to having intelligent insights. Losing predictive capabilities feels like losing car knowledge they've grown to depend on.
