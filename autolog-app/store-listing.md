## Description

Track your car's entire story — every service, fill-up, and dollar spent — with beautiful insights that help you drive smarter.

Car Story is the free, privacy-first vehicle maintenance tracker that turns your repair history into actionable intelligence. No ads. No account required. Your data stays on your device.

▸ SMART GARAGE
• Track unlimited vehicles with photos, mileage, and VIN
• Instant demo data on first launch — see insights right away
• Automatic maintenance schedule recommendations

▸ SERVICE & FUEL LOGGING  
• Log oil changes, tire rotations, brake jobs, and any service
• Track fuel fill-ups with MPG/efficiency calculations
• Attach photos of receipts and repairs
• Record vendor, cost, mileage, and notes

▸ COST INTELLIGENCE
• See your cost per mile and compare against industry benchmarks
• Monthly cost summary — shareable with friends
• Spending breakdown by category (donut chart)
• Spending activity heatmap (GitHub-style calendar)
• Month-over-month and year-over-year trend comparison
• Estimated annual cost forecast with confidence indicators
• Separate recurring maintenance from major repair costs

▸ RECALL ALERTS
• Automatic safety recall checks via NHTSA database
• Dismiss or hide alerts per vehicle

▸ DATA OWNERSHIP
• Export all data as JSON — you own your data
• Generate printable HTML reports per vehicle or fleet
• Dark & light mode
• Imperial (mi/gal) or metric (km/L) units
• Multi-currency support (USD, EUR, GBP, KRW)

▸ WHY CAR STORY?
• 100% free — no ads, no subscriptions, no paywalls
• 100% private — zero data collection, no analytics
• Works offline — no internet required for core features
• Beautiful data visualizations — not just another spreadsheet

Whether you have one daily driver or a whole fleet, Car Story helps you understand what your vehicles actually cost — and when they need attention.
