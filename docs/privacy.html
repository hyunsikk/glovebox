Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Privacy First: Car Story is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect no personal information, and all your vehicle and maintenance data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect NO personal information. Car Story does not require account creation, email registration, or any personal identifiers. You can use the app completely anonymously.

1.2 Vehicle and Maintenance Data

All vehicle information and maintenance records you enter into Car Story are stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Vehicle make, model, year, and VIN
  • Maintenance schedules and service records
  • Photos you attach to service records
  • Cost and vendor information
  • Custom notes and reminders

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it using our export feature, which creates local files on your device.

1.3 Usage Analytics

We do not collect any analytics or usage data through our own systems. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs beyond what is included with AdMob (see Advertising section below).

2. Advertising

2.1 AdMob Integration

The free version of Car Story displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information to provide personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA/Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

2.2 Google's Privacy Policy

AdMob is operated by Google. Their data collection and use practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which you can review at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.3 Ad Personalization

You can control ad personalization through your device's privacy settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

2.4 Ad-Free Experience

Upgrading to Car Story Pro removes all advertisements and eliminates any data collection by AdMob.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your vehicle and maintenance data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS data storage mechanisms. This data is:

  • Protected by your device's security features
  • Included in your device's encrypted backups
  • Not accessible to other apps
  • Never transmitted to our servers

3.2 Data Export

When you use our export feature, we create local files on your device that you can save, share, or backup as you choose. We do not have access to these exported files.

3.3 App Deletion

If you delete the Car Story app, all your data is permanently removed from your device. We cannot recover this data since we don't store it on our servers.

4. Information Sharing

We do not share your information with anyone because we don't collect personal information in the first place. The only data collection that occurs is through Google AdMob for advertising purposes in the free version of the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Car Story is not intended for children under 13. Since we collect no personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Rights

6.1 Your Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Modification: Edit or update any information directly in the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual records or clear all data from the app settings
  • Portability: Export your data using our export feature

6.2 No Data Requests Needed

Since we don't store your personal data on our servers, there's no need to request data access, modification, or deletion from us. You have direct control over all your information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

If we make material changes that affect how we handle data, we will notify users through the app or other appropriate means.

8. Third-Party Services

Aside from Google AdMob (for advertisements in the free version), Car Story does not integrate with any other third-party services that could access your data.

9. International Users

Car Story is developed and operated in the United States. Since we collect no personal data (except for AdMob's collection as described above), there are no international data transfers to be concerned about regarding your vehicle and maintenance information.
