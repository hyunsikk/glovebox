Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data stays on your device. Car Story never uploads your vehicle information, service records, or personal data to any server. Your data is as private as the photos on your phone.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings → Export Data to download a JSON file with all your vehicles, services, and fuel logs. You can also generate a printable HTML report from Settings → Reports. Your data is also included in iCloud/device backups.

Can I track multiple vehicles?

Yes! Car Story supports unlimited vehicles. Add as many as you need from the Garage tab. The Insights tab shows combined or per-vehicle analytics.

How do recall alerts work?

Car Story checks the NHTSA (National Highway Traffic Safety Administration) recall database using your vehicle's make, model, and year. Only this vehicle information is sent — no personal data. You can hide or dismiss individual recall alerts.

How are cost benchmarks calculated?

Cost benchmarks are based on published industry averages from AAA, RepairPal, and Edmunds. They show how your maintenance spending compares to the national average for your vehicle's make.

Can I switch between miles/km or USD/EUR?

Yes — go to Settings to change distance units (imperial/metric) and currency (USD, EUR, GBP, KRW). All displays update automatically.

Is Car Story really free?

Yes, completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions. All features are available from day one.

What happens if I delete the app?

All data is permanently deleted. We recommend exporting your data first (Settings → Export Data). Since we don't store anything on servers, deleted data cannot be recovered.
