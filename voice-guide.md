# Glovebox Voice Guide

## Voice Identity: Your Car-Savvy Friend

**Tone:** Confident, knowledgeable, efficient. Like a trusted mechanic who explains things clearly without talking down to you.

**Key Principles:**
- Uses proper automotive terminology
- States facts, makes recommendations, avoids corporate speak
- Shorter is always better
- Sounds like ONE person across all interactions

---

## Voice Examples by Context

### **1. Success States**

#### **Maintenance Completed:**
✅ **"Oil change logged. Next one due March 2024."**

*Not:* "Congratulations! Your oil change has been successfully recorded in your maintenance history. We'll send you a friendly reminder when your next service is approaching!"

#### **Reminder Set:**
✅ **"We'll remind you next Tuesday."**

*Not:* "Great! You've enabled notifications for this maintenance item. You'll receive a helpful reminder notification."

### **2. Error States**

#### **Car Not Found:**
⚠️ **"Can't find that exact model. Try '2023 Toyota RAV4' instead."**

*Not:* "Oops! We weren't able to locate that vehicle in our database. Please try entering your vehicle information again."

#### **Network Issue:**
⚠️ **"Can't load service centers right now. Your maintenance data is safe."**

*Not:* "We're experiencing connectivity issues. Please check your internet connection and try again."

### **3. Empty States**

#### **No Vehicles:**
🚗 **"Ready to add your first car?"**

*Not:* "Welcome to Glovebox! To get started with tracking your vehicle maintenance, please add your first vehicle to your garage."

#### **No Service History:**
📝 **"Add your last oil change to see when you're due next."**

*Not:* "You haven't logged any maintenance yet. Start building your service history by adding your most recent maintenance."

### **4. Push Notifications**

#### **Maintenance Due:**
🛢️ **"RAV4 oil change due next week"**

*Not:* "Don't forget! Your 2023 Toyota RAV4 is scheduled for an oil change service next week."

#### **Cost Insight:**
💰 **"Your brake pads are lasting 20% longer than typical"**

*Not:* "Good news! Based on our analysis, your brake pad wear rate is below average for your vehicle type."

### **5. Paywall/Premium**

#### **Multi-Vehicle Limit:**
🚙 **"Track unlimited vehicles for $2.99/month"**

*Not:* "Upgrade to Premium to unlock the ability to manage multiple vehicles in your Glovebox account!"

#### **Advanced Analytics:**
📊 **"See cost predictions and insights with Premium"**

*Not:* "Premium members get access to our advanced analytics dashboard with detailed cost forecasting."

### **6. Loading States**

#### **Searching Vehicles:**
🔍 **"Finding your car's maintenance schedule..."**

*Not:* "Please wait while we search our comprehensive vehicle database for your specific model information."

#### **Calculating Costs:**
💰 **"Analyzing your service history..."**

*Not:* "Our system is processing your maintenance data to provide personalized cost estimates."

### **7. Milestone/Achievement**

#### **First Year Complete:**
🎉 **"One year of smart maintenance tracking complete"**

*Not:* "Congratulations on your first anniversary with Glovebox! You've successfully maintained your vehicle for a full year."

#### **Money Saved:**
💵 **"You've saved $340 vs dealer pricing this year"**

*Not:* "Based on our calculations, your choice of service providers has resulted in significant savings compared to dealership rates."

### **8. Gentle Nudges**

#### **Overdue Maintenance:**
⏰ **"Your oil change is running 2 weeks late. Still plenty safe."**

*Not:* "Your vehicle maintenance is overdue. While this isn't immediately critical, we recommend scheduling service soon."

#### **Pattern Recognition:**
🔍 **"Your air filter is getting dirty faster than expected"**

*Not:* "Our system has detected that your air filter replacement frequency is above the typical range for your vehicle model."

### **9. Data Insights**

#### **Cost Trending:**
📈 **"Your maintenance costs are trending 15% below average"**

*Not:* "Compared to other similar vehicles in our database, your maintenance expenditures are performing favorably."

#### **Wear Pattern:**
🛞 **"Front tires wearing evenly. Good alignment."**

*Not:* "Based on your tire rotation history, your wheel alignment appears to be within normal specifications."

### **10. Account/Settings**

#### **Data Export:**
📤 **"Service records sent to your email"**

*Not:* "Your maintenance history has been successfully exported and delivered to your registered email address."

## Voice Guidelines

### **DO Use:**
- **Specific automotive terms:** "20K service" not "regular maintenance"
- **Clear timelines:** "next week" not "soon"
- **Confident statements:** "Your engine can handle another 1K miles" 
- **Manufacturer authority:** "Toyota recommends..." not "We suggest..."
- **Precise data:** "15% below average" not "doing well"

### **DON'T Use:**
- **Corporate speak:** "We're excited to..." "Thanks for choosing..."
- **Hedging language:** "might," "could," "perhaps"
- **Chatbot energy:** "Great question!" "Happy to help!"
- **Unnecessary pleasantries:** "Hope you're having a great day!"
- **Technical jargon:** API errors, system messages

## Personality Consistency Check

### **The Voice Test:**
Read any two messages together. Do they sound like they came from the same person?

**Example Pair:**
✅ **"Oil change logged. Next one due March 2024."**
✅ **"Your brake pads are lasting 20% longer than typical."**

*These sound like the same knowledgeable person talking about different aspects of car maintenance.*

### **The Friend Test:**
Would you say this to a friend who asked about their car?

**Good:** "Your oil change is running late. Still plenty safe."  
**Bad:** "Your maintenance is overdue. Please schedule service promptly."

### **The Confidence Test:**
Does this make the user feel more confident about their car maintenance decisions?

**Good:** "Toyota recommends 10K mile intervals for your engine."
**Bad:** "You might want to consider changing your oil soon."

## Context-Specific Variations

### **First-Time User:**
- More explanatory: "Based on Toyota's specs, you've got 2K miles left"
- Build trust: "Real manufacturer data, not generic advice"

### **Experienced User:**
- More concise: "Due next week"
- Assume knowledge: "30K service includes spark plugs"

### **Premium User:**
- More advanced insights: "Brake wear suggests highway driving"
- Sophisticated language: "Cost variance analysis shows..."

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### **❌ Too Friendly/Chatty:**
"Hey there! Hope you're having a great day! Just wanted to let you know your car might need some attention soon! 😊"

### **✅ Appropriately Professional:**
"RAV4 oil change due next week"

### **❌ Too Technical:**
"API synchronization complete. Vehicle database query returned maintenance interval specifications."

### **✅ Clear and Informative:**
"Found your car's maintenance schedule"

### **❌ Generic/Vague:**
"Your vehicle requires maintenance soon. Please schedule an appointment."

### **✅ Specific/Actionable:**
"Oil change due Tuesday. $45-65 expected."

---

## International Considerations

### **Units:**
- Default to local standards (miles in US, km elsewhere)
- Currency symbols appropriate to region
- Date formats match local conventions

### **Automotive Terms:**
- "Hood" vs "Bonnet" based on region
- "Gas" vs "Petrol" appropriately
- Maintenance intervals match local manufacturer specs

---

## Voice Evolution

### **As Data Increases:**
More specific predictions replace general advice:
- **Month 1:** "Oil changes typically cost $45-65"
- **Month 6:** "Your oil changes usually cost around $52"

### **As Trust Builds:**
More direct recommendations:
- **Early:** "Consider scheduling your oil change"
- **Later:** "Schedule oil change next week to avoid rush pricing"

## Success Metrics

### **User Feedback Indicators:**
- "This app actually knows what it's talking about"
- "Feels like talking to someone who understands cars"
- "Straightforward and helpful, not pushy"

### **Behavioral Success:**
- Users follow recommendations without seeking second opinions
